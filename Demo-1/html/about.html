Our History

Form 2006 until Now

2006 - 2010

Our Track and Field Club (TFC) founded by Stoil Filiov at Double Oaks and Earle Village Recreation Centers. Anthony James (Coach AJ) named Head Coach.TFC hosted its first developmental meet at West Charlotte High School; 300 athletes participated.

2010 - 2014

TFC voiced its support to the Mecklenburg Board of County Commissioners for the construction of an outdoor track at Johnson C. Smith University. November 2010, Mecklenburg County park bonds were approved for construction of the track and field facility.TFC created its own Automatic Timing and Meet Management Team to time and manage high school, college and youth developmental meets, including the Jim Law Invitational.
